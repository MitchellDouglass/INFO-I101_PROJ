IN-WX Data Viewer

Visualizing historical weather warning data in Central Indiana, courtesy of the Indianapolis Weather Forecast Office.

About The Data Visualization

Navigate an interactive map of Central Indiana - featuring all of the counties served by the Indianapolis Weather Forecast Office. You'll be able to choose the year, and the type of warning you'd like to view, then simply hover over a county to see the total count!

A Bit About Our Data

Our data is sourced directly from the National Weather Service through an online program provided by the Iowa State University, known as the Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM). This provides easy access to the data for anyone who wants to use it.
